About

Hello! My name is Sam Bakenhus, and I go by the online handle Squid Jelly. I grew up all across the midwest of the United States but currently reside in Rochester, NY. Since I was young I've loved all varieties of creative work but I naturally gravitated toward one thing, and I always knew that's what I wanted to do.

It's now been over 10 years that I've enjoyed working on games whether it's been for solo projects, collaborations, or contract. I've worked for big global companies down to small apartment studios building for Mobile, Web, and PC. I hold experience in nearly every aspect of game development and have worked in many different genres. I'm comfortable in a wealth of different programming languages and libraries, working with graphics, physics, logic, networking and AI. I consider myself very self-motivated and a fast learner. I pride myself in being a generalist game developer, bringing whatever is needed to the project.

I believe we'll continue to see games grow and dominate as an artform in the years to come and I'm very much looking forward to being a part of it.
